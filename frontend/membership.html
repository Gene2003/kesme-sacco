FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about joining and using KESME SACCO.

Who can join KESME SACCO?
KESME SACCO is open to any Kenyan resident aged 18 and above — including smallholder farmers, agribusiness entrepreneurs, SME owners, youth, women groups, cooperative societies, and individuals seeking to improve their financial wellbeing.
How soon can I access a loan after joining?
New members can apply for a loan after 3 months of consistent savings contributions. Emergency loans may be available after 1 month for qualifying members. The loan amount you qualify for is based on your savings history and ability to repay.
What interest rate do you charge on loans?
Interest rates vary by product type and member history, ranging from 12% to 18% per annum on reducing balance. Agribusiness and group loans may attract preferential rates. Contact us for your personalised rate based on your specific loan needs.
Can a group or chama join as a single member?
Yes. We have a Group Savings Account and Group Boost Financing specifically designed for chamas, self-help groups, and table banking groups. Groups can save collectively and access group loans. Individual group members can also open personal accounts separately.
Is my money safe with KESME SACCO?
KESME SACCO is regulated by the SACCO Societies Regulatory Authority (SASRA) and operates under the SACCO Societies Act. Member deposits are safeguarded, and the SACCO maintains prudential standards set by the regulator. All accounts are covered by the deposit protection framework.
Can I withdraw my savings at any time?
Regular savings can be partially withdrawn, subject to maintaining the minimum balance required to qualify for products. Fixed deposits can only be accessed at maturity or with a penalty for early withdrawal. We encourage consistent saving to build your loan qualification history.
How do I repay my loan?
Loan repayments can be made via M-Pesa to our Paybill number, direct bank transfer, or cash at our office. For agribusiness loans with seasonal repayment, we work with you to set a repayment schedule that aligns to your harvest or income cycle. Details are provided at loan disbursement.
