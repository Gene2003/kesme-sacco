About KESME SACCO

Learn who we are, what drives us, and why thousands of Kenyans choose us as their financial partner.

Who We Are

Built on Purpose, Driven by People

KESME SACCO is a dynamic, member-driven Savings and Credit Cooperative dedicated to empowering individuals, groups, and agribusiness enterprises through accessible financial services, capacity building, and market-driven solutions.

Our Vision

To be a leading SACCO in Kenya driving inclusive growth and transforming livelihoods through sustainable financial services — reaching every smallholder, every entrepreneur, every community.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and innovative financial solutions that empower members to grow their incomes, build assets, and achieve long-term financial stability — through savings, credit, and capacity building.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make and every member we serve.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability at every level.

Transparency

Open books, clear processes, and honest communication with all members.

Member Focus

Every product, policy, and decision is made with member wellbeing at the center.

Innovation

Continuously developing new products and leveraging technology to serve members better.

Sustainability

Building long-term financial and environmental sustainability for members and communities.

How We Work

Our Integrated Approach

We combine three powerful pillars to deliver real, lasting economic impact for our members.

01

Finance

Providing accessible savings, credit, and investment products designed around the realities of small entrepreneurs and farmers — including seasonal repayment schedules, low entry barriers, and competitive rates.

02

Training & Capacity Building

Equipping members with financial literacy, business planning skills, record keeping, and credit management knowledge — turning financial access into real economic growth and long-term resilience.

03

Market Linkages

Connecting members to structured commercial markets, buyers, and agribusiness value chain actors — ensuring produce reaches markets at fair prices and members participate in profitable commercial systems.

Our Members

Who We Serve

KESME SACCO serves a diverse, inclusive membership committed to financial growth.

Smallholder Farmers

Providing seasonal credit, input financing, and market linkages to boost farm productivity and income.

Agribusiness Entrepreneurs

Supporting those along the agricultural value chain — processors, traders, and input suppliers.

Youth & Women Groups

Empowering marginalized groups with targeted financial products and capacity building support.

SMEs & Cooperatives

Fuelling small and medium enterprises and cooperative societies with working capital and development loans.
