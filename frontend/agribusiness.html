Agribusiness & Value Chains

Empowering farmers, cooperatives, and agribusiness entrepreneurs with finance, training, and market access.

Supporting Agriculture

Finance That Follows
the Farming Cycle

Agriculture is the backbone of Kenya's economy and the primary livelihood for millions of families. Yet farmers and agribusiness actors consistently face barriers to finance — rigid repayment schedules, lack of collateral, and limited market access.

KESME SACCO bridges this gap with tailored agribusiness financing products that align with production cycles, support the full value chain, and are packaged with training and market linkage support.

From smallholder farmers in Machakos to cooperative societies and agribusiness SMEs, we design our products to match the real rhythms of agricultural production — not the other way around.

Our Focus Areas

Value Chains We Support

We work across key agricultural value chains where our financing can have the greatest impact.

🥑

Avocado Value Chain

Kenya is a top avocado exporter. We finance farmers from seedling to export — covering inputs, post-harvest handling, packaging, and market access to premium international buyers.

🌿

Horticulture

Supporting vegetable, fruit, and flower growers with input financing, irrigation equipment credit, cold storage access, and connections to domestic and export markets.

🐓

Poultry & Livestock

Credit for chicks, feeds, veterinary costs, and housing — with links to processors and offtakers to ensure your poultry and livestock enterprise is commercially viable.

🌾

Cereals & Grains

Seasonal credit for maize, beans, and other cereal farmers aligned to planting and harvest timelines, with access to aggregated market channels and storage solutions.

🍵

Cash Crops

Supporting coffee, tea, macadamia and other cash crop farmers with medium-term credit for plantation maintenance, processing equipment, and value addition activities.

🐟

Aquaculture & Fisheries

Financing for fish ponds, fingerlings, feeds, and equipment — helping rural communities tap into the fast-growing aquaculture sector for improved nutrition and income.

Our Offer

Three Pillars of Agribusiness Support

We deliver an integrated package — not just money, but the tools and connections to succeed.

Financing

  • Input financing for seeds, fertiliser & pesticides
  • Seasonal credit aligned to harvest cycles
  • Equipment and irrigation credit
  • Post-harvest handling & storage loans
  • Value addition and processing support
  • Working capital for agribusiness SMEs

Training & Capacity Building

  • Good Agricultural Practices (GAP)
  • Post-harvest management training
  • Farm business management
  • Financial literacy for farmers
  • Record keeping and costing
  • Cooperative governance training

Market Linkages

  • Access to export-ready buyers
  • Collective marketing facilitation
  • Off-take agreement support
  • Market price information systems
  • Cold chain and logistics connections
  • Trade fair and buyer meetings
Why KESME for Agribusiness

We Understand Agriculture

Unlike conventional lenders, we design products around the realities of farming — not banking norms.

Seasonal Repayment Schedules

We align loan repayments to your harvest or production cycle — so you repay when your income comes in.

Flexible Collateral

We work with group guarantees, produce warrants, and savings-backed credit — no need for formal land title.

Group & Individual Products

Whether you farm alone or as part of a cooperative, we have a solution that matches your structure.

Embedded Market Linkages

Our agri-products come with built-in market access support — connecting you to buyers before you produce.

Technical Training Included

All agri-loan clients access free training in GAP, post-harvest handling, and farm business management.

Competitive Rates

We offer lower rates than most microfinance institutions, especially for group borrowers with good credit history.
