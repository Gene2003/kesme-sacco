Products & Services

What We Offer

Our Financial Products

Choose the right financial solution for your journey — from savings to credit to capacity building.

Regular Savings Account

A flexible, easy-to-use savings account that earns interest on your deposits. Ideal for all members.

  • Minimum monthly contribution from KES 500
  • Competitive interest rates on savings
  • Access to loan based on savings history
  • No monthly maintenance fees

Savings

Fixed Deposit Account

Lock your savings for a fixed period and earn higher interest returns. Perfect for building capital.

  • Terms from 3 to 24 months
  • Higher interest than regular savings
  • Guaranteed returns on maturity
  • Can be used as loan collateral

Savings

Group Savings Account

Designed for chamas, self-help groups, and table banking groups to pool resources and grow together.

  • Supports groups of 5 to 50+ members
  • Transparent group account management
  • Access to group boost financing
  • Regular group financial statements

Group

Development Loan

Long-term financing for business expansion, asset acquisition, and enterprise development projects.

  • Up to KES 5,000,000
  • Repayment up to 36 months
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Flexible collateral requirements

Credit

Emergency Loan

Fast-tracked loans for urgent personal and family needs — processed within 24 hours for qualified members.

  • Disbursed within 24 hours
  • Up to 3x your savings balance
  • Minimal documentation required
  • Repayment up to 12 months

Emergency

SME Business Loan

Tailored financing for small and medium enterprises — covering working capital, inventory, and expansion.

  • For registered and unregistered SMEs
  • Working capital and asset financing
  • Business advisory support included
  • Linked to capacity building training

SME

Input Financing

Credit for purchasing seeds, fertilisers, pesticides, and other agricultural inputs at the start of the season.

  • Linked to certified input suppliers
  • Repayment aligned to harvest period
  • Supports avocado, horticulture & more
  • Available for individuals and groups

Agriculture

Seasonal Credit

Short-term credit products aligned to agricultural production cycles, with repayment after harvest.

  • Harvest-aligned repayment schedules
  • Covers production and post-harvest costs
  • Renewable each agricultural season
  • Track record builds credit profile

Seasonal

Value Chain Financing

Comprehensive financing across the agricultural value chain — from farm to market, supporting every actor.

  • Avocado & horticulture value chains
  • Poultry and livestock support
  • Market linkage embedded in product
  • Access to structured off-take agreements

Value Chain

  • Available in Swahili and English
  • Regular refresher workshops
  • Free for all active members

Training
